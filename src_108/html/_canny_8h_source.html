1 /*
2  * Class: Canny
3  * Author: Pasquale Barile
4  *
5  * The Canny class applies a Canny edge detection filter.
6  *
7  * First smooth the image to eliminate any noise.
8  *
9  * Then find the image gradient to highlight regions with high spatial
10  * derivatives.
11  *
12  * Then track along these regions and suppress any pixels that are not a local
13  * maximum.
14  *
15  * Then further reduce the array through hysteresis in order to find pixels
16  * that missed suppression.
17  *
18  * The hysteresis use two thresholds: T1 and T2. Below T1 signifies no edge,
19  * above T2 signifies an edge. Between T1 and T2 signifies no edge unless a path
20  * can be found to a gradient above T2.
21  */
